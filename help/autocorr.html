Definition
Correlations between two values of the same variable at equispaced times.

The 95% confidence level is indicated by two horizontal lines. If the series is completely random, the probability is roughly 95% that the correlation falls within the lines. The ordinates of the lines are at `2/sqrt(N)` , where N is the number of data points.
Parameters
In the top slot, the qualitative field to be analayzed. The values must be sorted by the time.
