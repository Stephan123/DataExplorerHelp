Definition
Visual representation of the cross-tabulation of two categorical fields.
Parameters
In the top slot, the qualitative field represented in column. In the left slot, the qualitative field represented in line.
Options
Option 1: the area of the rectangle in color at the intersection of a line and column is proportional to the number represented. Negative numbers have opposite color.
Option 2: the area of the black rectangle is proportional to the theorical value computed from the marginal profiles.
Option 3: balloon plot : the area of the circle is proportional to the number represented.
