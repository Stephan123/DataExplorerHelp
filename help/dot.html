Definition
Circular graphic, divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportion.
The origin of the graphic is at the top (twelve o'clock). The slices are ordered clockwise, by decreasing size.
As it is difficult to compare different slices, this representation should be used only when comparing the slices to the total.
Parameters
In the top slot, the qualitative field whose categories are to be represented.
Options
Option 1: Normal pie chart. The area of each slice is proportional to the weight of the corresponding category.

Option 2: Equidistributed pie chart : each slice has the same size.

Option 3: Doughnut chart.
