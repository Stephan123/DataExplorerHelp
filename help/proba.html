Definition
Plot the data against a theoretical distribution so that the points should be along a straight line.

On the horizontal axis : ordered statistic medians, i.e. quantiles of `(i-0.3175)/(n+0.365)` where `i` is the point number.
On the vertical axis : ordered values of the numerical field.

The correlation coefficient associated with the linear fit measures the goodness of the fit.
The intercept and slope give estimates of the location and scale parameters of the distribution.

Parameters
In the top slot, the qualitative field to analyze.

Actions
Choose the distribution from the popup menu.
