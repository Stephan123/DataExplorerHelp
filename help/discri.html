Definition
The first two factors , linear combination of the original variables, that best separate the categories of the qualitative field, are computed
Then the data points are represented in the cartesian coordinate system of these two factors.
For each group, the ellipses show the points at Mahalanobis distance 0.5, 1, 1.5 and 2 from the center of the group.
Parameters
In the right slots, the quantitative fields used as coordinates of the data points.
In the left slot, the qualitative field used to class the data points.
In the right slot, an optional qualitative field used to label the points.
Options
Option 1: data points in the cartesian coordinate system of the first two factors.
Option 2: circle of correlation of the variables with the first two factors.
Option 3: histogram of the eigenvalues, i.e. the parts of variance explained by the factors.
Actions
Drag a numerical field to the graph to change its unit. The points will be represented as bubbles of size proportional to the numerical value.
